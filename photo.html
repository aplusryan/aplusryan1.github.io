Photography

Hello!

Welcome again! Chances are you already learned a bit about me and my passion for coding. Another one of my passions is photography. I initially told you that creating was my passion, and photography is in line with that statement. Whenever I take a photo, I don't only feel like I captured the canvas, but I feel like I added to the picture with my angles, techniques, and editing. DSLR's are quite expensive, so I stuck with taking pictures with my iPhone. Don't underestimate the power of an iPhone camera, these cameras are still quite good. I initially started to take photos on my iPhone 6s, but then I later moved on to the iPhone XS (thanks mom & dad). I edited most of my photos on my iPhone and on Pixlr. As you may see, I am still a beginner but I am trying my best! Enjoy the pictures!
